Like Mike

About the Film

Like Mike is one of my favourite movies because it mixes basketball, comedy, and heart. It tells the story of a young boy who finds a pair of magical shoes that make him play basketball like a superstar.

Image source: Wikipedia – Like Mike

Why I Like It

I like this movie because it has many funny moments and shows how confidence and teamwork can make a person amazing. People were amazed at how a player could go from average to incredible.
